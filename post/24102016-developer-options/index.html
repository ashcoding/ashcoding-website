Removing Developer options

October 24, 2016

Let’s say you are developing or debuging on an Android phone that you don’t own. After messing around with Developer options, you’ll want to remove that from the Settings in Android. But how?

Well, seems like the solution is to Clear data from the Settings.

Here’s how: 1. Go to Settings > Apps > Settings 2. Press Clear data 3. Done!
